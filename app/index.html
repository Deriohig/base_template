A bit about me

Irish born and raised, growing up in Dun Laoghaire - a seaside town of south Dublin. Adventurous, creative, and mad about travel, I live life to the fullest and enjoy having a bit of a laugh (once all the work is done) :-)

Im an enthusiastic web developer who's interests lie in creating beautiful, simplistic, and user-friendly web experiences. Be that cutting edge frameworks or old school static sites, I enjoy creating.

After graduating with a first class honors from I.A.D.T Dun Laoghaire I jumped straight into working at Software Design where I experienced a variety of different projects, some of which are featured on this site.

A front-end developer at heart but more frequently full-stack, I've experience in delivering pixel perfect solutions to client requirements. Always looking to develop my skillset, I'm confident in being thrown in the deep end while continuously learning and striving for improvement.
